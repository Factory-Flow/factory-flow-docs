---
title: "Define products and BOMs"
description: "Create SKUs and bills of materials so work orders have the right structure."
---

Before you release a work order, define what you build and what it consumes.

## Create a SKU

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a product">
    Navigate to **Inventory** and click **Create SKU**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture key fields">
    Provide a name, SKU code, and any unit or packaging details your team uses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Build the bill of materials

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a BOM">
    Open the SKU and choose **Create BOM**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add components">
    Add the parts or sub-assemblies required for the SKU, including quantities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Optional: inventory baselines

If you track on-hand inventory, set starting quantities so consumption reports are accurate.
