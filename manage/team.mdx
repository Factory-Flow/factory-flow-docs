Every person who uses Factory Flow in your organization is a member with a defined role. Admins manage who can join, what they can do, and who they are.

## Viewing Members

Navigate to **Organization Settings → Members** to see everyone currently in your organization. The list shows each person's name, email address, role, and when they last signed in.

## Inviting Someone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Members">
    Go to **Organization Settings → Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Click the **Invite Member** button in the top-right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email and role">
    Enter the person's email address and select the role they should have (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). See the role descriptions below if you're unsure.

    <Frame>
      <img className="block dark:hidden" src="/images/manage/team-invite-modal.png" alt="Invite Member modal with email input and role selector" />
      <img className="hidden dark:block" src="/images/manage/team-invite-modal-dark.png" onError={(e) => { e.currentTarget.src = "/images/manage/team-invite-modal.png"; }} alt="Invite Member modal with email input and role selector" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invitation**. The person receives an email with a link to accept. If they don't already have a Factory Flow account, they'll be prompted to create one as part of accepting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pending invitations appear below the members list. You can resend or revoke a pending invitation at any time.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Everything — manage members, billing, API tokens, gateways, alert groups, and all configuration. |
| **Editor** | Create and edit machines, lines, facilities, shifts, layouts, and alert rules. Cannot manage team or billing. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to analytics, layouts, machines, lines, and alert history. Cannot create or change anything. |

<Tip>
  Use **Viewer** for anyone who only needs to read dashboards and reports — supervisors, executives, or external auditors. Use **Editor** for engineers and operators who need to configure machines and alerts.
</Tip>

---

## Changing a Member's Role

Click the role badge next to any member's name to open the role selector. Changes take effect immediately.

---

## Removing a Member

Click the **•••** menu next to any member and select **Remove from Organization**. Their account is not deleted — they simply lose access to this organization. You can re-invite them at any time.

<Warning>
  You cannot remove the last Admin from an organization. Assign another member as Admin first.
</Warning>

---

## Joining Without an Invitation

If your organization is set to **Public** (toggled under Organization Settings → General), users can search for your organization by name and submit a join request. Admins see pending requests on the Members page and can approve or decline them.

---

## Alert Groups

Alert groups are email distribution lists used for notifications. They are separate from organization membership — you can add any email address to an alert group, including addresses that don't have a Factory Flow account.

Manage alert groups under **Organization Settings → Alert Groups**.
