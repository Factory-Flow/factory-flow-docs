All self-hosted Factory Flow deployments require an active license key. Without a valid license, the application will enter a restricted mode.

## Activating Your License

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain your license key">
    Your license key is provided by Factory Flow when you purchase or renew a self-hosted subscription. Check your purchase confirmation email or your account portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Application Settings">
    Sign in as an App Admin and navigate to **Application Settings → License**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the license key">
    Paste your license key into the input field and click **Activate**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm activation">
    The page will display your license details — expiry date, seat limit, and enabled features. A green status indicator confirms the license is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## License Details

After activation, the License page shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Status** | Active, Expired, or Invalid. |
| **Expiry Date** | When the license needs to be renewed. |
| **Seat Limit** | The maximum number of organization members allowed across your deployment. |
| **Features** | Which Factory Flow features are enabled under this license. |

---

## Renewing a License

When your license approaches expiry, you will see a warning banner in Application Settings. To renew:

1. Purchase a renewal through your Factory Flow account or by contacting sales.
2. You'll receive a new license key.
3. Navigate to **Application Settings → License** and enter the new key.

<Note>
  You can activate a new license key at any time before the current one expires. The new expiry date takes effect immediately on activation.
</Note>

---

## License Enforcement

| Status | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Active** | Full access to all licensed features. |
| **Expiring soon** | Warning banner shown in Application Settings; full access continues. |
| **Expired** | Application enters read-only mode. New data ingestion is paused until renewed. |
| **Invalid** | Application will not start. Verify the key is entered correctly. |

---

## Air-Gapped Environments

License validation is performed locally — Factory Flow does not contact external servers to verify your license. Your deployment can operate fully offline once installed and activated.
