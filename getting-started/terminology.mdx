## Core terms

- Facility: A physical site where production happens.
- Line: A sequence of stations that make a product.
- Station: A single step in the line.
- Machine: An asset that reports production signals.
- Layout: A versioned floor plan with machine placements.
- SKU: A product identifier.
- BOM: Components required to build a SKU.
- Work order: A production order for a quantity of a SKU.
- WIP: Work in progress.
- Hold: A paused state due to quality or material issues.
