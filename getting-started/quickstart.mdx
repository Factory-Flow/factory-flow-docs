This quickstart stays short on purpose. Each step links to a focused page so you can move quickly without a long scroll.

## The path in five steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your account and organization">
    Create your account, choose a plan, and invite teammates. See [Account setup](/getting-started/account-setup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model your factory">
    Create a facility, lines, machines, and shifts. See [Factory setup](/getting-started/factory-setup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define what you build">
    Add SKUs and BOMs so work orders have structure. See [Define products and BOMs](/getting-started/define-products).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect machine data and verify dashboards" stepNumber={4}>
    Send a test payload and validate analytics. See [Connect machine data](/getting-started/connect-data) and [Verify dashboards](/getting-started/verify-dashboards).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you will need

- Admin access to create facilities and machines
- At least one production line and machine
- A test SKU and simple BOM (even a one-step BOM is fine)
