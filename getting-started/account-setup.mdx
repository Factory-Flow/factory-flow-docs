This guide walks you through the complete account setup process for Factory Flow, from creating your account to configuring your organization.

## Creating your account

### Sign up options

Factory Flow supports multiple authentication methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email (Passwordless)">
    1. Navigate to [app.factoryflow.com](https://app.factoryflow.com)
    2. Enter your email address
    3. Click **Continue**
    4. Check your inbox for a magic link
    5. Click the link to sign in (valid for 15 minutes)

    <Tip>
      Passwordless login is secure and eliminates the need to remember passwords. A new link is sent each time you sign in.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Google">
    1. Click **Continue with Google**
    2. Select your Google account
    3. Authorize Factory Flow to access your profile information
    4. You'll be signed in automatically

    Works with personal Gmail accounts and Google Workspace.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Microsoft">
    1. Click **Continue with Microsoft**
    2. Enter your Microsoft credentials
    3. Authorize the application
    4. You'll be redirected back to Factory Flow

    Supports Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure AD) for enterprise SSO.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="GitHub">
    1. Click **Continue with GitHub**
    2. Authorize Factory Flow
    3. You'll be signed in with your GitHub identity

    Useful for development teams and technical users.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Email verification

If you sign up with email, you must verify your address before accessing all features:

1. Check your inbox for a verification email
2. Click **Verify Email Address**
3. You'll be redirected to Factory Flow with a confirmation

<Warning>
  If you don't see the verification email, check your spam folder. You can request a new verification email from the login screen.
</Warning>

## Organization onboarding

After signing in for the first time, you'll complete the organization onboarding process.

### Option 1: Create a new organization

If you're starting fresh:

1. Click **Create Organization**
2. Enter your organization name
3. Choose a subscription plan
4. Complete payment setup (if applicable)
5. You'll be taken to your new organization dashboard

### Option 2: Join an existing organization

If you've been invited to an organization:

1. Accept the invitation from your email, or
2. Search for the organization by name
3. Request to join
4. Wait for an admin to approve your request

<Note>
  Pending invitations are shown on the onboarding screen. Click **Accept** to join immediately if you've been invited.
</Note>

## Inviting team members

Once your organization is set up, invite your team.

### Sending invitations

1. Go to **Organization Settings** (click your organization name in the sidebar)
2. Click **Members**
3. Click **Invite Member**
4. Enter the email address
5. Select a role:
   - **Viewer** - Read-only access to dashboards and reports
   - **Editor** - Create and modify machines, lines, layouts, and shifts
   - **Admin** - Full access including billing, users, and API tokens
6. Click **Send Invitation**

### Managing invitations

Pending invitations appear in the Members list with a "Pending" status. You can:

- **Resend** - Send another invitation email
- **Revoke** - Cancel the invitation before it's accepted

### Handling join requests

If someone searches for your organization and requests to join:

1. You'll receive a notification
2. Go to **Organization Settings → Members**
3. Review the request
4. Click **Approve** or **Deny**

## Configuring organization settings

### General settings

Navigate to **Organization Settings** to configure:

- **Organization name** - Update your organization's display name
- **Default settings** - Configure timezone and default preferences

### Security settings

Depending on your plan, you can configure:

- **Allowed authentication methods** - Restrict sign-in to specific providers
- **Session duration** - Control how long users stay logged in
- **IP restrictions** - Limit access to specific networks (enterprise plans)

## Managing your profile

### Accessing profile settings

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**

### Updating your profile

- **Name** - Your display name shown across Factory Flow
- **Email** - Contact support to change your email address
- **Appearance** - Choose light or dark theme

### Changing your password

If you use email/password authentication:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Update Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Save**

## Switching organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations:

1. Click your organization name in the sidebar
2. Select **Switch Organization**
3. Choose the organization to switch to

Or use the organization switcher in the header navigation.

## Leaving an organization

If you need to leave an organization:

1. Go to **Organization Settings → Members**
2. Find your name in the member list
3. Click **Leave Organization**
4. Confirm your decision

<Warning>
  If you're the last member of an organization, leaving will delete the organization and all its data. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Deleting your account

To delete your Factory Flow account:

1. Leave all organizations (or transfer ownership if you're the last admin)
2. Contact support at [support@factoryflow.io](mailto:support@factoryflow.io)
3. Request account deletion

<Note>
  Account deletion is permanent and removes all your personal data. Organization data is only affected if you're the last member.
</Note>
