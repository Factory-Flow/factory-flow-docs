---
title: "Verify dashboards"
description: "Confirm data is flowing and analytics are updating."
---

Once data is flowing, validate it in the UI to ensure analytics are accurate.

## Check the machine status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the machine detail">
    Go to **Machines**, select your machine, and confirm the status is **Connected**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Live Data">
    Open the **Live Data** tab to see recent payloads and timestamps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review analytics dashboards

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboards">
    Navigate to **Analytics → Dashboards** and verify cycle time and throughput tiles update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate KPIs">
    Review OEE or downtime metrics to ensure your thresholds are realistic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If data is missing or delayed, check [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) for common causes.
</Tip>
