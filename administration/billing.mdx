Factory Flow billing is handled through Stripe, providing secure payment processing and subscription management. This guide covers how to manage your subscription, update payment methods, and access invoices.

## Subscription overview

Each Factory Flow organization requires an active subscription to access core features.

### Subscription states

| State | Description | Feature access |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Trial** | Evaluation period | Full access |
| **Active** | Paid subscription | Full access |
| **Past due** | Payment failed | Limited access (grace period) |
| **Canceled** | Subscription ended | Limited access until period ends |
| **Expired** | No active subscription | Restricted access |

### What subscriptions include

- Access to all Factory Flow features
- Machine data ingestion and analytics
- Team member access (based on plan)
- Support and updates

## Managing your subscription

### Accessing billing settings

1. Navigate to **Organization Settings**
2. Click **Billing** or **Subscription**
3. View your current plan and status

### Subscription information

The billing page shows:
- Current plan name
- Subscription status
- Next billing date
- Payment method on file

## Stripe billing portal

Factory Flow uses Stripe's customer portal for billing management.

### Accessing the portal

1. Go to **Organization Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Manage Billing** or **Billing Portal**
3. You're redirected to Stripe's secure portal

### Portal capabilities

In the Stripe portal, you can:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **View invoices** | Access all past invoices |
| **Download receipts** | Get PDF receipts for accounting |
| **Update payment method** | Change credit card or payment details |
| **Update billing info** | Change billing address and email |
| **View subscription** | See current plan details |
| **Cancel subscription** | End your subscription |

### Updating payment method

1. Access the Stripe billing portal
2. Click **Payment methods**
3. Add a new payment method
4. Set as default
5. Optionally remove old methods

### Downloading invoices

1. Access the Stripe billing portal
2. Click **Invoices** or **Billing history**
3. Find the invoice you need
4. Click **Download** for PDF

## Trial period

New organizations start with a trial period.

### During trial

- Full access to all features
- No payment required immediately
- Trial duration shown in billing settings

### Trial ending

Before your trial ends:
1. You'll receive email reminders
2. Add a payment method to continue
3. Choose a subscription plan

### After trial

If no payment method is added:
- Access becomes restricted
- Data is preserved
- Reactivate by subscribing

## Subscription plans

Factory Flow offers multiple plans to fit different needs.

### Comparing plans

Plans differ by:
- Number of machines
- Data retention period
- Support level
- Advanced features

### Changing plans

To upgrade or downgrade:

1. Access the Stripe billing portal
2. Click **Subscription**
3. Select a different plan
4. Confirm the change

Changes take effect:
- **Upgrades** - Immediately
- **Downgrades** - End of current billing period

## Canceling your subscription

### How to cancel

1. Access the Stripe billing portal
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

### What happens after cancellation

- Access continues until the end of the billing period
- No further charges
- Data is preserved during this time
- You can reactivate before the period ends

### Data after cancellation

- Data is retained for a grace period
- Export important data before expiration
- Contact support for data export assistance

### Reactivating

To resume your subscription:

1. Go to **Organization Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Resubscribe** or **Reactivate**
3. Confirm payment method
4. Full access is restored

## Payment issues

### Failed payments

If a payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Factory Flow retries the charge
3. Update your payment method if needed
4. Access may be limited after multiple failures

### Updating after failure

1. Access the Stripe billing portal
2. Update your payment method
3. Stripe will retry the charge
4. Access is restored when payment succeeds

### Grace period

After a failed payment:
- Limited access during grace period
- Time to update payment method
- Full access restored on successful payment

## Invoices and receipts

### Invoice delivery

- Invoices sent to billing email after each charge
- Available in Stripe portal anytime
- PDF format for accounting

### Invoice contents

Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Plan details
- Amount charged
- Payment method (last 4 digits)

### Tax information

- Tax calculated based on billing address
- Update address in Stripe portal for accurate tax
- Contact support for tax exemption

## Billing notifications

Factory Flow sends billing emails for:
- Payment confirmations
- Failed payment alerts
- Subscription changes
- Trial ending reminders

### Managing notifications

Billing emails go to:
- Organization billing email (configurable)
- Organization admins

## Security

### Payment security

- Payments processed by Stripe (PCI compliant)
- Card details never stored by Factory Flow
- Secure encryption for all transactions

### Access control

- Only Admins can access billing settings
- Billing portal requires authentication
- Changes logged for audit

## Common questions

### Can I pay annually?

Yes, annual plans are available. Contact sales or check the billing portal for options.

### Can I get a refund?

Contact [support@factoryflow.com](mailto:support@factoryflow.com) to discuss refund requests.

### Can I change billing email?

Update the billing email in the Stripe portal under billing information.

### Do you offer discounts?

Contact sales for volume discounts, non-profit pricing, or special programs.
