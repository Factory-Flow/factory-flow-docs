Your profile settings let you manage everything about your personal Factory Flow account — your name, security settings, notification preferences, and appearance.

Navigate to **Settings** using the user menu in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar.

## Profile

Update your display name, email address, and contact information. Your display name is shown in the members list and audit log within your organization.

To update your email address, you will be sent a verification link to confirm the new address before it takes effect.

---

## Password

Click **Change Password** to update your password. You'll need to enter your current password before setting a new one.

<Tip>
  Use a unique, strong password for Factory Flow. Consider a password manager to generate and store it securely.
</Tip>

---

## Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds a second layer of security to your account. When enabled, signing in requires both your password and a one-time code from an authenticator app.

**To enable 2FA:**
1. Go to **Settings → Security**.
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**.
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, etc.).
4. Enter the code shown in your app to confirm setup.
5. Save your **recovery codes** in a safe place — these are one-time codes you can use if you lose access to your authenticator app.

<Warning>
  Recovery codes can only be used once and are shown only at setup time. Store them securely (e.g. in a password manager). If you lose both your authenticator device and your recovery codes, you will be locked out of your account.
</Warning>

## Active Sessions

The **Sessions** section shows all devices where you are currently signed in, including the browser, operating system, and approximate location.

Click **Revoke** next to any session you don't recognize to immediately sign that device out. Use **Revoke All Other Sessions** to sign out everywhere except your current device.

---

## Alert Notifications

The **Alert Notifications** section shows all alert groups you are a member of. You can **mute** individual groups to stop receiving alert emails from them without removing yourself from the group. This is useful when you're on leave or temporarily don't need a specific stream of notifications.

---

## Appearance

Toggle between **Light** and **Dark** mode. The preference is saved to your account and applies across all devices.

---

## Danger Zone

The **Delete Account** option permanently removes your Factory Flow account and all personal data associated with it. You will be removed from all organizations you are a member of.

<Warning>
  If you are the only Admin of an organization, you must assign another member as Admin before you can delete your account.
</Warning>
