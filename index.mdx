## Features

- **Real-Time Machine Monitoring** – Connect any machine with an incrementing part counter to instantly see live production data. Track parts produced, machine status, and performance as it happens — no manual data entry required.
- **OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)** – Measure true machine productivity with a single, industry-standard score. Drill into the three pillars — **Availability**, **Performance**, and **Quality** — to pinpoint exactly where losses occur.
- **Cycle Time Analysis** – Track how long each part takes to produce. Visualize cycle time trends and distributions over time, set thresholds to flag abnormal cycles, and identify bottlenecks before they impact output.
- **Automatic Downtime Detection & Categorization** – Downtime is detected automatically when a machine stops producing parts. Events are categorized and tracked so you can see exactly how long machines are idle, how often, and why.
- **Production & Throughput Tracking** – Monitor output against expected targets in real time. View production forecasts, compare actual vs. planned throughput, and track quality vs. scrap rates across any time window.
- **Pareto Analysis** – Identify which downtime causes have the greatest impact on your operation. Focus improvement efforts where they matter most using 80/20 breakdowns of loss events.
- **Multi-Level Analytics** – Analyze performance at any level of your operation — across the entire organization, by facility, by production line, or down to individual machines. Drill from overview to detail in one click.
- **Shift Scheduling** – Define when machines are expected to run. Shift data is used to correctly calculate OEE and availability, ensuring metrics reflect actual planned production time — not 24/7.
- **Production Line & Facility Management** – Group machines into production lines and facilities. Manage your factory hierarchy and see how performance rolls up from machine to line to site.
- **Factory Floor Layouts** – Build a visual map of your factory floor. Place machines on a layout blueprint so your team can quickly navigate to the machine they care about.
- **Flexible Data Ingestion** – Send part count data via a simple REST API or through a **Factory Flow Gateway** — an edge device that collects data locally and syncs it, even in low-connectivity environments.
- **Analytics Export** – Export any analytics dataset to CSV or Excel for reporting, further analysis, or sharing with stakeholders.
- **Team & Access Management** – Invite team members to your organization with role-based access — **Viewer**, **Editor**, or **Admin** — so everyone sees what they need and nothing more.
- **Self-Hosted Deployment** – Run Factory Flow entirely on your own infrastructure. Includes license management, SMTP configuration, LDAP/Active Directory integration, and in-app system updates.
