---
title: "Factory setup"
description: "Create your facility, lines, machines, and shifts so production has a structure."
---

Use this page to build the minimum factory model needed for your first run.

## Create a facility

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your facility">
    Go to **Facilities** and click **Create Facility**. Provide a name and (optionally) a location or description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define areas or zones">
    If you track multiple areas, add zones or areas now so reporting stays organized.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add a production line

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a line">
    Navigate to **Production Lines** and click **Create Line**. Assign it to your facility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order the stations">
    Add the stations or sequence your machines to reflect material flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add machines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create machines">
    Go to **Machines** and click **Create Machine** for each station. Assign the facility and line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set initial cycle time targets">
    In the **Analytics** tab, set an expected cycle time to enable performance visuals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assign shifts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create shifts">
    Open **Scheduling** and add the shifts that match your production calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Associate shifts with lines or machines">
    Assign shifts so capacity planning and analytics reflect actual staffing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
