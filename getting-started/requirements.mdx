## Accounts and roles

- Admin access for initial configuration.
- Editor access for production scheduling and execution.
- Viewer access for read-only dashboards.

## Devices

- Tablets or workstations on the floor.
- Barcode scanners if you plan to scan work orders or inventory.
 - Machine gateways or devices that can post to the ingestion API.

## Data to gather

- Facility and line names.
- Machine list with identifiers.
- SKU list and basic routing.
- Initial inventory on hand.
