## What is Factory Flow?

Factory Flow is a production monitoring platform that connects to your machines and gives your team a single, clear view of how your factory is performing — right now and over time.

From a real-time overview of every machine on the floor to historical OEE trends across your entire facility, Factory Flow turns raw machine data into insights your team can act on immediately.
